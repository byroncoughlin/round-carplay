motoCarPlay

A round-display CarPlay dashboard for a 1976 BMW R75/6

A Raspberry Pi 5 drives an 800×800 circular screen that mirrors CarPlay in the center while wrapping it with live motorcycle telemetry read from a custom sensor pack — GPS speed & heading, lean angle, G-force, pitch, altitude, and dual cylinder-head temperatures. Tap any gauge to open its full-screen history graph. The dashboard below is the real UI, running in your browser and driven by a simulated ride.
